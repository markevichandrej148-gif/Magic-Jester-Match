Developer Narrative & Value Proposition

Welcome to the core marketing vision deck for Magic Jester Match. My name is Orest Daniv, and I am an aspiring independent developer fully dedicated to building premium quality, high-performance mobile software for the iOS marketplace.

Current Product Commercialization Status: Magic Jester Match is being deployed completely free of charges. The initial launch profile does not carry forced monetization scripts, paid subscription structures, or active in-app purchase modules.

Product Roadmap & Continuity Plans: To sustain ongoing development, deploy advanced levels, and maintain reliable architecture updates, I intend to deploy balanced in-app purchases and voluntary ad placements in forthcoming releases. This long-term commercial blueprint will support the product, create an organic revenue channel, and allow me to continue delivering great content.

My constant objective is to embed exceptional aesthetic value and deep user utility into mobile software by optimizing core layout engines and providing a pristine user experience across all Apple retina standards.
